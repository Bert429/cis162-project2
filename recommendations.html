List of personal recommendations of tracks, albums, and artists.

Harbor EP, Tomppabeats

This album is what introduced me to the genre way back in 2017. It is a classic of the genre, and every track is worth a listen. In my opinion, this album is what lofi is.

Koto City, Mama Aiuto

This has been a favorite of mine since late 2020. It takes a different approach from typical chillhop, but it's a very fun listen.

Monster Gambling in Tokyo, Idealism

Idealism is a popular artist within the genre. Low fidelity has a strong presence in his tracks, and this one is no exception. They play with environmental sounds a lot as well, which I enjoy.
